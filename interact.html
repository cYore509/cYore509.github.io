Community is at the forefront of the fight against cancer. See how you can get involved here.

The American Cancer Society

Ways To Contribute:

  • Start or join a making strides team
  • Attend local Galas and Balls
  • Drive patients to and from treatment
  • Volunteer at local making strides or relay for life events

The American Cancer Society is the largest non profit charity dealing with cancer. Along side funding research on treatments and cures they stand out for their commitment to survivors. Offering programs like Road To Recovery which helps patients to get the treatment they need and be involved in the community as to not be alone in their fight.
