Learn

Learning and understanding cancer as a disease and its treatments is important for everyone.

  • Cancer cells grow and spread uncontrollably
  • Cancer can be found anywhere in the body
  • Cancer occurs in up to 1 in 3 people
  • Commonly you will see Breast (Woman), Prostate (Men) or Lung (Both)
  • Stages of cancer determine the spread of the cancer
  • Common Treatments: Surgery, Radiation, Chemotherapy
  • Advances in science and technology are creating less aggressive treatment options
